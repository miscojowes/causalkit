Insurance Dataset - Variable Descriptions
Network: 27 variables, 52 directed edges
Source: Binder et al. 1997 (sampled from Bayesian network)
Data points: 10000

GoodStudent: Good student (discount)
Age: Age category of policyholder
SocioEcon: Socioeconomic status
RiskAversion: Risk aversion level
VehicleYear: Year of vehicle (Current/Older)
ThisCarDam: Damage to this car
RuggedAuto: Ruggedness of automobile
Accident: Accident occurred
MakeModel: Make and model of car
DrivQuality: Driving quality
Mileage: Annual mileage
Antilock: Antilock brakes present
DrivingSkill: Driving skill level
SeniorTrain: Senior training completed
ThisCarCost: Cost of this car
Theft: Car theft occurred
CarValue: Car value
HomeBase: Home base security
AntiTheft: Antitheft device present
OtherCarCost: Cost of other car
OtherCar: Other car in household
MedCost: Medical cost
Cushioning: Cushioning quality
Airbag: Airbag present
ILiCost: Income loss cost
DrivHist: Driving history
PropCost: Property cost
